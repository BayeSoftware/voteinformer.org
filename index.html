What is VoteInformer?

VoteInformer is a civic engagement app that connects citizens with information they need to participate meaningfully in the political process. Our app invites you to vote on the same topics that the government has already decided on, offering a clear comparison of how your views align with those of political parties. Whether youâ€™re exploring how parties align with your values, reviewing easy-to-understand legislative summaries, or analyzing your voting profile, VoteInformer makes navigating the political landscape more approachable and allows you to explore dynamic insights through interactive tools. We aim to support informed decision-making and make political engagement not only more accessible but also enjoyable. Our alpha versions are currently available for both Canada and the United States, and are designed to deepen your understanding of how your values map onto the political landscape.
